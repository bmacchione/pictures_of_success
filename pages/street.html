Tonight the Streets are Ours

I love the song that I stole the title from to name this section. It's such a good theme song for anyone up to any sort of art in the streets at night. I also love when I have the opportunity to wander a city at night. You never know what you'll find. These shots are all from Seattle. The combination of the darkness and all the artificial lights make a lot of night city shots almost cinematic. My favorite photos usually have multiple possible storylines in them.
