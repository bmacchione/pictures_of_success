Snow Dayze

Here are some photos from when it snowed on Bainbridge in January. I love taking photos in the snow. It's so silent and gorgeous out. These photos were all taken with Kodak Portra 160 color film. A few of the shots almost seem black and white because they're so monochromatic. No other light is like snow light, and it's somewhat rare to get to see here.
